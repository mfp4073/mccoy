  9. [S73] Obituary of Elmer Clyn Gilbert. Elmer Clyn Gilbert, son of Frank and Martha Gilbert was born in Jasper County, Iowa, near Prairie City, April 21, 1879, and passed away at his home in Milford on Tuesday, March 14, 1944, at the age of 64 years, 10 months and 21 days. His death came as a great shock to his family, as he had apparently been in good health. Mr. Gilbert grew to young manhood in the vicinity of Audubon and Guthrie county, Iowa, and moved with his parents to Pocahontas county in the year 1909. He was united in marriage to Paulina Pearson on September 21, 1910 near Havelock, Iowa. To this union two sons were born; Myron and Dale Gilbert both of Milford. Mr. and Mrs. Gilbert moved to Milford in 1912 and he was employed by Cass Myers at the elevator for several years when first married. They moved to a farm near Mallard, Iowa where he farmed until returning to Milford in 1937. He then worked as a carpenter for Anderson Lumber Co. Mr. Gilbert was a member of both the Masonic and Knights of Pythias lodge and took great pleasure in his lodge work. Those of his immediate family to mourn his passing are his wife, two sons, one brother, Royce Gilbert of Spirit Lake, Iowa, two sisters, Elmay Scheffers of Pella, Iowa, Florence Buck of Alpha, Minn. Another sister Lulu Scheffers died March 6, 1930 at Dedham, Iowa. There are also five grandchildren who were always a source of great happiness and pride to Mr. Gilbert. Their love for him was proof of their mutual devotion. There is also a large number of nieces and nephews and friends in this community and in other place who will greatly miss him.
  10. [S84] Obituary of Elmer Clyn Gilbert. E.C. Gilbert, a resident of this community for the past 21 years, passed away at his home in Milford Tuesday evening, March 14, about 6 o'clock after an illness of only one day. Mr. Gilbert, a carpenter by trade, had been employed by the Anderson Lumber Company for several years. It was while at his work Monday afternoon that he was stricken with a heart attack. He was able to get to his home, but failed to respond to treatment. Elmer Clyn Gilbert was born April 21, 1879, in Jasper county, Iowa, son of Mr. and Mrs. Franklin Pierce Gilbert. He passed away at his home in Milford Tuesday, March 14, 1944 at the age of 64 years, 11 months. He is survived by his wife, Pauline; two sons, one brother, Royce Gilbert of Spirit Lake and two sisters, Mrs. Fred Buck of Alpha, Minn., and Mrs. Elma Scheffers of Pella, Iowa. Funeral services will be held Friday afternoon at 2:30 at the Methodist church. Rev. J.O. Smith will have charge of the last rites. Burial will be in Milford cemetery.
