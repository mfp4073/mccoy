Mary Douglas Benham1

F, b. 11 January 1907, d. 10 October 1988
     Mary Douglas Benham was born on 11 January 1907 at Chicago, Cook Co., Illinois.2,3,4 She was the daughter of James William Benham and Alice Douglas Bradford. Mary Douglas Benham was adopted after July 1914. Shortly after July 1914 her name was legally changed to Mary Brizard. She married Francis Andrews Galligan on 9 November 1929 at Eureka, Humboldt Co., California.4 Note.5 Mary Douglas Benham married Commander Claren Emmett Duke before 14 April 1945 at San Francisco, San Francisco Co., California.6,7 Mary Douglas Benham died on 10 October 1988 at San Francisco, San Francisco Co., California.3 She was buried in December 1988 at Mountain View Cemetery, Oakland, Alameda Co., California, 37.83379,-122.24045.8,3
