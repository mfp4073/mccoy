Samuel Hull

M, b. 9 February 1754, d. 30 September 1835
     Samuel Hull was born on 9 February 1754 at New Haven, New Haven Co., Connecticut. He was the son of Ebenezer Hull and Lydia Dunbar. Samuel Hull married Mabel Bradley, daughter of Griffin Bradley and Mabel Thompson, on 15 March 1779 at New Haven, New Haven Co., Connecticut. Samuel Hull died on 30 September 1835. Note.1

Citations

  1. [S565] Source not known, Samuel Hull served in the Revolutionary Way as a private on the Continental Line and the Connecticut Line from 1777 through 1779. For a short time he was listed as a deserter, but afterwards rejoined the 3rd Regiment and was honorably discharged. Both he and his wife, Mabel/Mehitable, received a pension.
