Colfax, Iowa -- Funeral services for Frieda Nadine Cross, 79, of Colfax, will be held Saturday at 1 p.m. at the Howard Street Christian Church with the Rev. Jill Flyr and the Rev. Tom Burns officiating. Burial will be at Colfax Cemetery. Visitation will be held after noon Friday at the Walters-Coburn Funeral Home in Colfax where family will be present from 6 to 8 p.m. Friday. Memorials to the Howard Street Christian Church or Mercy Hospice will be accepted. Mrs. Cross died Tuesday, June 9 at Mercy Hospital Medical Center in Des Moines. The daughter of Roy Philip and Mable Blanche Romans Griffioen, she was born April 6, 1919, in Colfax. She was a 1936 graduate of Colfax High School. She married Royal E. Mrs. Cross was a member of the Ladies Reserve Officer Association, Howard Street Christian Church, OES, Colfax Federated Woman's Club, American Legion Auxiliary, Christian Women's Fellowship, and Women's Golf League. Survivors include her husband; a son and daughter-in-law, Doug and Karen of Colfax; two grandchildren; and a sister, Betty Jacobson of Mitchellville. She was preceded in death by her parents, an infant daughter, a sister and an infant brother.
