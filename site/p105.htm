Arthur F. Hagendorf

M, b. 31 January 1864, d. August 1925
     Arthur F. Hagendorf was born on 31 January 1864 at Waverly, New Jersey.1 He was the son of Carl Ludwig Hagendorf and Clara Eliese Wohlfarth.2 Arthur F. Hagendorf married Laura Ellen McCoy, daughter of William Washington McCoy and Eliza Jane Brewin, on 16 August 1894 at Brooklyn, Poweshiek Co., Iowa.1,3,4 Arthur F. Hagendorf married Anna Marie Moser on 16 June 1915.1,5 Arthur F. Hagendorf died in August 1925 at Albany City Hospital, Albany, Albany Co., New York.5 He was buried at Ames, New York Cemetery.6
