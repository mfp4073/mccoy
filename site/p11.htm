William Russell Benham1

M, b. 22 September 1823, d. 30 May 1907
     William Russell Benham was born on 22 September 1823 at Bridgewater, Oneida Co., New York. He was the son of Sedgwick Benham and Betsy Burnett.2 William Russell Benham was born on 27 September 1823 at Bridgewater, Oneida Co., New York.1 He married Helen Maria Bingham, daughter of John Bingham and Lucy Gage, on 19 June 1849 at Trinity Episcopal Church, Rochester, Monroe Co., New York.1 William Russell Benham died on 30 May 1907 at Toston, Broadwater Co., Montana.1
