Stephen Trowbridge Hastings

M, b. 22 June 1848, d. 6 April 1869
     Stephen Trowbridge Hastings was born on 22 June 1848.1 He was the son of Charles Hastings and Julia Trowbridge. Stephen Trowbridge Hastings died on 6 April 1869 at Birmingham, Oakland Co., Michigan.1 Obituary.2

Citations

  1. [S207] Francis H. Hastings, Family Record of Dr. Seth Hastings Senior of Clinton New York, page 129.

Trowbridge Hastings, son of the late Charles Hastings of Troy, Mich., in the 23d year of his age. He was an earnest worker in the church and Sabbath school, but "The Lord had need of him."
