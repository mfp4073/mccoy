Clarence Cecil Main

M, b. 24 August 1894, d. 22 December 1980
     Clarence Cecil Main was born on 24 August 1894 at Prairie City, Jasper Co., Iowa.1,2,3 He was the son of Lewis Main and Mary Elizabeth Gilbert. Clarence Cecil Main married Jessie Belle Willis, daughter of Henry Willis and Sarah Jane Banks, on 1 November 1915 at Newton, Jasper Co., Iowa.1,2,3 Clarence Cecil Main died on 22 December 1980 at Des Moines General Hospital, Des Moines, Polk Co., Iowa.1,2,3 Obituary.4,5 He was buried on 24 December 1980 at Colfax Cemetery, Colfax, Jasper Co., Iowa.3

He was preceded in death by his parents, wife, four sisters and two brothers. The son of Lewis and Mary Elizabeth Gilbert Main, he was born August 24, 1894, at Prairie City, Iowa. He attended rural schools and was a life-long resident of Colfax, where he was a trucker, farmer and carpenter by profession. On November 1, 1915, he was united in marriage to Jessie Willis at Newton, Iowa. He had resided in Colfax until a few years ago when he became a resident of the Altoona Nursing Home.
