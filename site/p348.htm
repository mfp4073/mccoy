  • Carrie Elizabeth Snelling b. 9 Oct 1865
  • Marion Belle Snelling b. 12 Apr 1868

Citations

  1. [S207] Francis H. Hastings, Family Record of Dr. Seth Hastings Senior of Clinton New York, page 89.
  2. [S207] Francis H. Hastings, Family Record of Dr. Seth Hastings Senior of Clinton New York, page 89. Dr. Snelling was the son of Col. Josiah Snelling, who was born in Boston, 1782, and died in Washington, D. C., 1829, aged 47 years. He was a soldier of the U. S. Army, serving with distinction in the War of 1812. He was afterward in command at several important posts on the western frontier. In 1824, Ft. Snelling, which was named in his honor by Gen. Winfield Scott, was completed under his direction. Col. Snelling married Abby Hunt, daughter of Col. Thomas Hunt, of Kentucky.
