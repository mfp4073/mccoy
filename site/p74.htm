In 1845 he was married to Susanna Brunner. Seven children; Sarah, Mary A., Edwin C., Reuben H., John T. and James Milton. The latter died at the age of six. After 14 years of married life his wife died and in 1859 he married Susan R. Peckinpaugh, who died March 13, 1903. They were the parents of seven children also; Emma J., Nancy, Julia, Ida Belle, Allen S., Olive and Susanna. Allen S. and Nancy have died. He moved from Indiana to Knox county, Illinois in 1852 and in 1869 he came to Jasper county, settling on a farm near Prairie City until 1892 when he moved to town.
