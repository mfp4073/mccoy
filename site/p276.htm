Charles Harrison Slankard

M, b. 6 November 1858, d. 21 June 1931
     Charles Harrison Slankard was born on 6 November 1858 at San Juan Batista, San Benito Co., California.1 He was the son of Harrison Slankard and Maria Fields. Charles Harrison Slankard married Minnie Kitridge Beauchamp, daughter of Causten Van Ranster Beauchamp and Sara Elizabeth Hinton, on 26 December 1882 at Phoenix, Maricopa Co., Arizona.1 Charles Harrison Slankard died on 21 June 1931 at Phoenix, Maricopa Co., Arizona.1 He was buried on 23 June 1931 at Double Butte Cemetery, Tempe, Maricopa Co., Arizona.1
