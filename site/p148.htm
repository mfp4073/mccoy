Citations

  1. [S167] Death of Earl Snookie Gilbert. Newberg Boy Fatally Hurt In Car Skid. Newberg, Sept. 28. -- Earl Gilbert, 16, Newberg, was injured fatally shortly before 9 p.m. Saturday in a traffic accident about a mile south of Dundee. Three other youths in the car, driven by Gilbert, were uninjured. According to the passengers, Gilbert apparently was blinded by the bright lights of a car approaching from the opposite direction. He swerved off the pavement and the car skidded about 185 feet before turning over. He died about an hour after being admitted to Willamette hospital here. Passengers in the car were Jack Heckert, route No. 2, Newberg; Robert Beasley, route No. 1, Newberg, and Kelly Kaller, route No. 3, Sherwood. The dead youth is survived by his mother, Mrs. Mary Gilbert, who operates a beauty parlor here, and an older brother, Merlin.
