  2. [S22] Wedding of Bruce Allen Gilbert. The Prairie City News, page 2. Cheryl Lynn Hill, the daughter of Norman and Helen Hill of Runnells, Iowa, became the bride of Bruce Allen Gilbert, son of Marvin and Mildred Gilbert of Prairie City, Iowa, on Saturday, June 9, at 5:30 p.m. The candlelight, double-ring wedding ceremony was held in the Christian Church of Prairie City. Officiating at the service were Pastor Marshall Myers of the Christian Church of Prairie City, and Pastor Tom Mabe of the First Baptist church in Colfax. The bride, given in marriage by her father and mother, wore a white satin gown featuring a sweetheart neckline and a sequined bodice of crystal organza. The short ruffled sleeves, bodice and high collar were accented by eye-catching crystals. Strands of pearl beads adorned the keyhole back of the fitted bodice. The flared skirt was embellished by rows of organza ruffles flowing to a chapel-length train. Her headpiece was a wreath of flowers and pearl beads. Her ensemble was completed by white satin gloves. The bride carried a cascading bouquet of pink-tipped roses and white carnations. Linda McGhee of Altoona, sister of the bride, was matron of honor and Bobbi Streeter of Prairie City, friend of the bride and groom, was bridesmaid. Dan Adams of Prairie City, friend of the groom, served as best man and Mike Streeter, also of Prairie City and friend of the bride and groom, was groomsman. Amanda Gilbert and Aaron Gilbert, niece and nephew of the groom, were flower girl and ring bearer. The bride's attendants wore burgundy satin, tea-length dresses and carried pink sweet-heart roses. The flower girl was attired in a pink satin tea-length dress. The attendants wore lace gauntlets. The groom wore a white tuxedo with tails. The groomsmen wore silver tuxedos with silver bow ties. The guests were seated by Scott Hill, brother of the bride, and Mike Gilbert, brother of the groom. Special music was provided by Nancy Garwick, soloist, who sang "The Wedding Song," "Time in a Bottle," and "Through the Eyes of Love." She was accompanied at the piano by Barb Steenhoek. The guest book attendant was Melissa Wemark. The host for the evening was David McGhee, brother-in-law of the bride, and the hostess was Sheryl Hill, sister-in-law of the bride. Following the ceremony, a reception was held in the basement of the church. Cake, coffee, and punch were served by Sharon Gilbert, Terri Gilbert, Brenda Van Ryswyk, and Melinda Schafman. Kayla Hill and Connie Terri, cousins of the bride, attended the gift table. The couple honeymooned in Vero Beach, Florida, and now reside in Runnells. He is employed by R & R Welding in Des Moines. Mrs. Gilbert is a nurse at Mercy Medical Center in Des Moines. A wedding rehearsal dinner was hosted by the groom's parents, Marvin and Mildred Gilbert, at the Prairie City Community Center on Friday, Jun 8.
