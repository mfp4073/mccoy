  • History of Sutton 1704 - 1876 History of the Town of Sutton, Massachusetts, From 1704 to 1876, compiled by Rev. William A. Benedict, A.M. and Rev. Hiram A. Tracy. This book contains some information on Col. Timothy Sibley. For example, see page 722. It also contains some genealogy information on his family and others.
  • Sibley Genealogy, Handwritten, Original The original handwritten version of the Genealogy of the Sibley Family by Derick Sibley, Cincinnati, May 22, 1858.
  • Sibley Genealogy, Handwritten, Copy A copy of the original Genealogy of the Sibley Family by Derick Sibley.
  • Sibley Genealogy, Typed, Version A This typed Sibley genealogy contains a subset of what is in the original handwritten genealogy; Genealogy of the Sibley Family. It tends to trace the side of the family that included the Benhams.
  • Sibley Genealogy, Typed, Version B This typed Sibley genealogy contains a subset of what is in the original handwritten genealogy; Genealogy of the Sibley Family. It tends to trace the side of the family that included the Derick Sibley Hartshorn III.
  • Sibley Genealogy, Typed, Version C This copy of the Sibley Genealogy contains typed additions originally handwritten in the Sibley Genealogy, Version A, genealogy (see above). It was probably created sometime after 1972 but before 1974.
  • Sibley, Derick, Genealogy This short five page typed genealogy following the ancestors of Derick Sibley was found in the original book; Genealogy of the Sibley Family.
  • Sibley Genealogy, Printed These pages (33-42) appear to have been cut from a larger unknown book. Starting on page 36 is the beginning of the "Genealogy of the Sibley Family."
