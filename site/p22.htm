Elizabeth Bowie Sneddon

F, b. 27 October 1908, d. 20 February 1995
Tib Sneddon
     Elizabeth Bowie Sneddon was born on 27 October 1908 at Bowie, Delta Co., Colorado. She was the daughter of James Adamson Sneddon and Mary Ann Bowie. Her common name was Tib. Elizabeth Bowie Sneddon died on 20 February 1995 at Paonia, Delta Co., Colorado. Obituary.1

Citations

  1. [S126] Obituary of Elizabeth 'Tib' Sneddon. Delta County Independent, ELIZABETH 'TIB' SNEDDON. Elizabeth Bowie "Tib" Sneddon, 86, a longtime resident of Paonia, died Monday, Feb. 20, in her home on Pitkin Mesa. She is survived by two nieces, Margaret A. Sneddon of Kalispell, Mont., and Nancy A. Murphy of Fort Worth, Texas; five nephews, Wallace A. Sneddon of Longmont, James A. Sneddon of Wheatridge, Chris Sands of Los Angeles, Calif., James D. Murphy Jr. of Mesa, Ariz., and David A. Murphy of Portland, Ore.; one sister-in-law, Evelyn Kepler Sneddon of Morrison; one brother-in-law, James D. Murphy Sr. of Fort Worth, Texas; several cousins and many close friends. Miss Sneddon was born in Bowie in 1908. Her childhood was spent between Bowie and Gallup, N.M. She was a graduate of Paonia High School and earned her bachelor's degree at the University of Colorado in Boulder and a master's degree at Columbia University in New York. Miss Sneddon was a professional educator. Her teaching career included junior high and high school in Paonia and Western State College in Gunnison. She was very active in the local community and held offices in several organizations; Delta Kappa Gamma, past president; Colorado Retired School Employees Association, vice president; Pitkin Mesa Club; American Association of University Women, past president; Friends of the Library, past president; North Fork Historical Society; Preservation Society; Sewing Club Friendship Guild (the former St. Margaret's Guild); Business and Professional Women; and sponsor of Alpha Sigma Alpha while teaching at Western State College. A memorial service for Miss Sneddon will be held on Saturday, Feb. 25, at 10 a.m. at Paonia High School. The family requests no flowers, but memorial contributions may be made to: North Fork Preservation Society, P.O. Box 96, Paonia, CO 81428; Paonia Public Library; Colorado Professional Business and Professional Women Foundation, in care of Corrin Campbell, 1410 Cherryvale Rd., Boulder, CO, 80303, public radio station KVNF; or the charity of donor's choice.

She had been a resident of Bear Creek Nursing Center in Morrison for two years. A private family memorial service for Mrs. Sneddon will be held at a later date. She was born in Delta on Oct. 7, 1915, to Ernest Lee and Ruth (Roberts) Kepler. Her childhood was spent on the Western Slop, attending summer camp on Grand Mesa and competing in various band competitions. She graduated from Delta high School and completed her education by earning a bachelor's degree in business from the University of Denver. During college she was an active member of the Sigma Kappa Sorority. Mrs. Sneddon worked for the federal government in Denver and Washington, D.C. Retiring in 1980, she relocated to Los Angeles, Calif., where she became involved as a teacher and a volunteer in a stroke rehabilitation class promoting independent living skill which was offered by the local community adult education center. She is survived by three sons and two daughters-in-law, Wallace A. and Maritza of Longmont, James A. and Kelly Sneddon of Wheat Ridge and Chris P. Sands of Los Angeles, Calif.; a daughter Margaret A. Evans Hospice, Inc., 3709 Evergreen Parkway, Evergreen, CO 80439; the Stroke Scholarship Fund, in care of Cal Eubanks, Torrance Adult School, Levy Adult Education Center, 3420 West 229th Place, Torrance, CA.
