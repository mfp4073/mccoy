[S565] Source not known, Speculation from Joan Carole (Sellers) Gilbert as told to Len Gilbert, April 2001: When my dad was very young (2 or 3?) and my grandmother was in Grass Valley, working, and not yet remarried, William Joseph showed up at the house, identified himself, then asked to take Leonard (my dad). The woman watching my dad phoned my grandmother at work. Grandma told her to slam the door in his face and tell him to leave. He did. My dad next saw his father in a coffin after he died in 1956. My dad went down for the funeral and I do have a photocopy of the memorial program. Only my dad, Silver Ivester, Robert Ivester, and Jackie Ivester are listed in as "Relatives Attending". No names are listed under "Others Attending". About 45 names are listed under "Friends who Called". 5 floral arrangements were sent. I also have copies of his birth certificate, death certificate, army discharge papers and some additional photos of him in uniform (not on Website). I also have a copy of a letter notifying the family that a contribution to the YMCA resulted in a plaque with Wm J. Gilbert's name that is in the lobby of the Wilmington YMCA building on Avalon St.
