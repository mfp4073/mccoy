Those left to honor his memory are his wife of over 58 years, Mary Alyce; a daughter, Kathy Eshelman of Des Monies; a son Danny Lee Clark and his wife Lanete of Hurst, Texas and five grandchildren, Tim, Nicolle and Rebecca Clark of Panora and Kari and Dru Eshelman of Des Moines.
