William Hull

M, d. 1849
     William Hull was born at Wallingford, New Haven Co., Connecticut.1 He was the son of Samuel Hull and Lois Peck. William Hull married Alma Hall, daughter of Reuben Hall.1 William Hull died in 1849. He was buried at Center Street Cemetery, Wallingford, New Haven Co., Connecticut.

F, b. 1782, d. 1849
     Eudocia Hull was born in 1782.1,2 She was the daughter of Gen. Andrew Hull and Elizabeth Mary Ann Atwater. Eudocia Hull married Hon. Samuel A. Foot.1 Eudocia Hull died in 1849.2
